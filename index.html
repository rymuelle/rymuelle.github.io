Hey!

Welcome to the personal site for Ryan Mueller. It's a collection of miscellaneous things I do.

Photography

Photography has been my nearly life long hobby of choice, and I hardly travel anywhere without a camera. You'll find a small set of photos semi-arbitrarily selected in the Photo Gallery.

Bread Baking

I almost love baking bread more than I like eating it, and I've been known as the "bread guy" at more than one location. I started baking after being inspired by a friend in grad school, and I particularly like baking lean doughs/french breads/sourdoughs like the one pictured above. You'll find some of my recipes on the bread page.

Photographic Denoising

I have yet to find an photographic denoiser that I really like, so I set out to build one myself! How I'm doing it can be found on the "Learned Demosaicing and Denoising RAW Images" page.

Photo Emulsion Project

By far my most ambitious project, I set out to create the first publicly accessible vegan film since the days of collodians. I have invented my own photographic emulsion that has useable sensitivity laboring for a year on my homemade double jet injection setup. Now, I just gotta get the thing to stick to plastic!
