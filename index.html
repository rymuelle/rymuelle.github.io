New release! Raw Refinery: an open source AI RAW denoiser

I have yet to find an photographic denoiser that I really like, so I set out to build one myself. Build on a light weight U-Net and trained on raw images from Canon, Nikon, and Sony cameras, Raw Refinery denoises the noisiest images while avoiding "plastic" look of other denoisers. The alpha release can be downloaded for Mac OS here.

Read more about Raw Refinery here.

Photo Emulsion Project

By far my most ambitious project, I set out to create the first publicly accessible vegan film since the days of collodians. I have invented my own photographic emulsion that has useable sensitivity laboring for a year on my homemade double jet injection setup. Now, I just gotta get the thing to stick to plastic!
